# Observation Phase

Guiding questions for each Paso during the observation phase of the coaching cycle.

## Paso 1 – Knowledge of Self

1. What aspects of your knowledge of self are you working on through this lesson?
2. What evidence can be gathered about your positionality in teaching?
3. What components of the lesson are informed by your knowledge of self?

## Paso 2 – Knowledge of Learner

1. Which students should be observed during the lesson?
2. What data can be collected about student performance?
3. How can patterns in student academic performance be investigated?

## Paso 3 – Lesson Plan / Teaching Practice

1. What should the observer focus on (student engagement, delivery, discourse, wait time)?
2. How will student success with the learning objective be monitored?
3. How will pacing and engagement be evaluated?

## Paso 4 – Sociopolitical Dynamics

1. What data will show whether students have equitable access to learning?
2. What data will show classroom power dynamics among students?
3. Which groups of students should be focused on for additional support?

## Paso 5 – Knowing Learners & Communities

1. How can engagement levels be observed for each student or group?
2. What evidence shows students connecting with the content?
3. How can classroom relationships and rapport be observed?

## Paso 6 – Advocacy

1. What evidence will show advocacy for equitable learning opportunities?
2. How can teacher talk support marginalized groups?
3. How can equitable participation be observed?
