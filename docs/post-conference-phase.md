# Post-Conference Phase

Guiding questions for each Paso during the post-conference phase of the coaching cycle.

## Paso 1 – Knowledge of Self

1. How did your positionality play a role in lesson delivery?
2. What did you learn about the content through teaching this lesson?
3. What did you learn about how students learn this content?
4. Where did you succeed in this lesson?
5. What would you adjust or do differently next time?

## Paso 2 – Knowledge of Learner

1. What additional information did you learn about student readiness?
2. In what ways were students successful?
3. Where did students struggle or need support?
4. What does the data suggest about next steps?

## Paso 3 – Lesson Plan / Teaching Practice

1. How did the lesson go overall?
2. What would you do differently next time?
3. What are your next steps for improving instruction?

## Paso 4 – Sociopolitical Dynamics

1. Did all students have equitable access to learning?
2. Were any groups marginalized or excluded?
3. How effective were strategies to support equity?

## Paso 5 – Knowing Learners & Communities

1. How did you connect with students during the lesson?
2. What additional information did you learn about students’ backgrounds?
3. How did students use their home languages to access the content?
4. How did the lesson build relationships?

## Paso 6 – Advocacy

1. What did you learn about student learning needs?
2. How will you advocate for support for students?
3. How will you communicate with families about learning?
4. What supports do you need moving forward?
